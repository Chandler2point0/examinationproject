Tupac Amaru Shakur

Quick Review About 2pac's Life And Career
Tupac Amaru Shakur was born Lasane Parish Crooks IN JUNE 16 1971 also known by his stage name 2PAC and briefly MAKAVELI, was an American rapper and actor. He is widely concidered one of the most influential and successful rappers of all time. Shakur is among the best-selling music artists, having sold more than 75 million records worldwide .Much of Shakur's music has been noted for addressing contemporary social issues that plagued inner cities, and he is considered a symbol of activism against inequality.
